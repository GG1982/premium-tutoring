Common Questions

Do you cover specific exam boards like AQA or Edexcel?

Yes. Our portal and tuition cover all major UK exam boards including AQA, Edexcel, and OCR. We tailor the lesson plan to your specific syllabus.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We work on a rolling monthly basis. We believe students should stay because they see results, not because they are locked in.

Do you teach small groups or is it 1-to-1?

We specialize in 1-to-1 private tuition to ensure maximum attention. However, we do offer small group revision courses during exam season.

How does the online portal work?

Every student gets a personal login. They can access thousands of questions, video solutions, and track their progress 24/7. It works on any tablet, phone, or laptop.

What if my child misses a lesson?

We understand family life is busy. We require 24 hours notice to reschedule a lesson without charge.
